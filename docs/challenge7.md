### Challenge 7: Plan future work

**Introduction:**

The first iteration is just the beginning. How can this agent be improved to provide even more business value?

**Description:**

Brainstorm and document future enhancements for the HR Training Agent. Consider new data sources, additional actions, or proactive notifications.

**Success Criteria:**

- A documented list of at least 3 future enhancements or features.

**Learning Resources:**

- Team brainstorming and user feedback.

**Tips:**

- Consider adding a Chat integration, proactive email reminders for overdue courses, or expanding the agent to handle overarching HR onboarding sequences.
